Notice on Research and Development Progress

Due to limited time and energy, TAI Research will temporarily adjust its research and development pace.

  • Regular updates: Small products and incremental improvements may still be released from time to time.
  • Major leaps: Large-scale upgrades (such as the transition from GTC-2 to GTC-3 Omni) will be postponed for now.
  • Internal focus: Our main energy during this stage will be invested in long-term foundational research projects, which require deep exploration and uninterrupted thinking.

This adjustment allows us to better balance current development with future breakthroughs.

We sincerely appreciate your understanding and continued support.
The journey of intelligence is long—every pause is to prepare for a greater leap forward.

🔥 GTC-Guard-0 Released — TAI Guardian's First Safety Model
We're proud to announce GTC-Guard-0, the first model in the TAI Guardian series. This lightweight text safety classifier (under 10M parameters) is built for interpretability and CPU inference.
